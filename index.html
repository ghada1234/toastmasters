About Toastmasters

Empowering Individuals to Become Confident Communicators and Leaders Toastmasters International is a nonprofit educational organization that teaches public speaking and leadership skills through a worldwide network of clubs. Since 1924, Toastmasters has helped millions of people find their voice, build confidence, and grow both personally and professionally. With more than 16,000 clubs in 145 countries, Toastmasters provides a supportive and positive learning environment where members can develop communication and leadership skills at their own pace.
